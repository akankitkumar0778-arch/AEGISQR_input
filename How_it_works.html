AegisQR turns your critical medical information into a scannable QR code — accessible by any first responder in seconds, no app or login required.

In an emergency, when every second matters and you can't speak for yourself, AegisQR speaks for you.

The Process
4 Steps to Full Protection

From sign-up to wearing your QR code, the entire process takes less than 10 minutes.

1

Create Your Free Account

Sign up with your email address or continue with Google. No credit card needed — AegisQR is completely free to use. Your account is secured with industry-standard encryption from the moment it's created.

Note: Your personal login credentials are never shared with anyone. Only the medical profile data you choose to make visible is accessible via your QR code.
2

Fill In Your Medical Profile

Enter your critical health information into your secure profile. This includes your blood type, known allergies, current medications, existing medical conditions, and emergency contact numbers. You can fill in as much or as little as you're comfortable sharing.

What you can include: Blood type · Allergies · Current medications · Chronic conditions · Organ donor status · Emergency contacts · Doctor's contact · Insurance details · Medical directives (e.g., avoid certain drugs, emergency instructions, DNR preferences).
3

Generate Your Personal QR Code

Once your profile is saved, AegisQR instantly generates a unique QR code linked to your medical profile. This code is yours permanently. Download it, print it, or add it to your phone's lock screen wallpaper.

Where people keep their QR code: Printed on a wallet card · On a medical ID wristband · As a phone lock screen · Stuck inside a helmet · On a keychain tag · On the back of a phone case.
4

First Responders Scan & Act Instantly

In an emergency, a paramedic, doctor, or bystander simply scans your QR code with any smartphone camera. They are immediately shown your medical profile — no app download, no login, no delay. The information they need is right there in seconds.

What responders see: Your name and photo · Blood type · Critical allergies · Active medications · Medical conditions · Emergency contact with phone number · Any special instructions you've written for responders.

Always Up to Date

Your QR code never changes — but your medical profile can be updated anytime from your account dashboard. Changed medications? New allergy discovered? Updated emergency contact? Simply log in, edit your profile, and save. Anyone who scans your QR code will always see your most current information. There is no need to reprint or redistribute anything.

Privacy & Security
Your Data. Your Control.

We take the privacy of your health information seriously. Here is exactly how we protect it.

Who can see my medical profile?

Only people who physically scan your QR code can view your profile. The link embedded in your QR code is unique and not indexed by search engines. It is not discoverable without the physical code itself.

Is my data encrypted?

Yes. Your data is securely encrypted and protected using industry-standard security practices. Your login password is hashed and is never stored in plain text.

Can I choose what information is visible on my QR profile?

Absolutely. You have full control over which fields appear on your public-facing emergency profile. You can hide any section you are not comfortable sharing while still keeping the rest visible.

Can I delete my account and data?

Yes. You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from your account settings. Once deleted, your QR code will no longer display any information.

Does AegisQR sell my health data?

Never. AegisQR does not sell, rent, or share your personal or medical data with any third party, advertiser, or partner. Your health information exists solely to help you in an emergency.
